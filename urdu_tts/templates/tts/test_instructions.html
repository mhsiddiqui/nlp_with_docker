Diagnostic Rhyme Test (DRT)

Click Play button and listen voice carefully. You can not play voice again so listen it carefully. You have to select word correct work in voice from list below.

Modified Diagnostic Rhyme Test (MRT)

Click Play button and listen voice carefully. You can not play voice again so listen it carefully. You have to select word correct work in voice from list below.

Mean Opinion Score (MOS)

Click Play button and listen voice carefully. You can listen voice multiple time. You have to rate voice according to quality of following properties.

  • Intelligibility: How much conveniently the word was recognized?
  • Naturalness: How close it seems to be produced by a human?
  • Overall: How do you rate this sound overall?
